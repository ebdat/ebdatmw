Welcome to EBDAT Consultancy: Your Partner in Data-Driven Success

Published on November 2, 2025 | By Mustafa Bwanali | Category: Welcome Remarks

Welcome to the official website of EBDAT Consultancy Limited! We are thrilled to launch our online presence and share our passion for data-driven solutions with you. As one of the founding members, I am incredibly proud of the team we have assembled and the services we offer. Our mission is to empower organizations like yours to make informed decisions by unlocking the power of data.

EBDAT Consultancy is a Malawian firm specializing in evidence-based data analytics, research, and consultancy. We bridge the gap between raw data and strategic decision-making, offering a range of services including data-driven research, performance monitoring and evaluation, and technology and digital solutions. Our multidisciplinary team of experts in data science, economics, and ICT is committed to delivering high-quality, actionable insights.

So, why choose EBDAT? We believe our strength lies in our unique combination of international standards and deep local context understanding. We are committed to an evidence-first approach, ensuring that every recommendation is backed by rigorous analysis. We don't just provide data; we provide insights that are practical, implementable, and tailored to your specific needs. We are not just a service provider; we are your partner in success.
