About EBDAT Consultancy Limited

Driven by Data. Powered by Evidence.

Our Story & Expertise

EBDAT Consultancy Limited is a Malawian evidence-based data and analytics firm specializing in research, consultancy, and data-driven solutions. We are dedicated to bridging the critical gap between raw data and strategic decision-making, empowering organizations and communities to make informed choices.

Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in data science, economics, and information and communication technology (ICT) to deliver high-quality, actionable insights. We work across government agencies, NGOs/development partners, and private sector companies, providing tailored solutions that enhance efficiency, measure impact, and drive sustainable development.

Our Detailed Process

We begin by engaging with your team to thoroughly understand your challenges, objectives, and the specific context of your project. This collaborative phase ensures that our solutions are perfectly aligned with your strategic goals.

Our experts design robust research methodologies, including data collection instruments, sampling strategies, and analytical frameworks, ensuring the highest levels of validity and reliability.

Leveraging advanced statistical software (R, SPSS, Stata) and cutting-edge analytical techniques, we transform raw data into meaningful patterns, trends, and insights.

We present our findings in clear, concise, and visually engaging reports, dashboards, and presentations, ensuring that complex information is easily digestible and actionable.

Our partnership extends beyond reporting. We provide strategic advisory and capacity building to help you effectively implement recommendations and achieve desired outcomes.

Why Our Malawi Focus Matters

EBDAT Consultancy is proudly Malawian, and our deep understanding of the local context, culture, and socio-economic landscape is a significant differentiator. This local expertise, combined with our commitment to international standards of research and analytics, allows us to deliver solutions that are not only technically sound but also culturally relevant and sustainable.

We believe that effective data-driven solutions must resonate with the realities on the ground. Our team's intimate knowledge of Malawi and the broader African region ensures that our insights are practical, implementable, and truly impactful for our clients and the communities they serve.
